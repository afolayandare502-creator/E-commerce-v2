1. How Long Do I Have to Make a Return?

We gladly accept returns on eligible items within 30 days of receipt, as long as the products are unused, unwashed, and still in resale condition with original tags and packaging where applicable.

2. What Items Can I Return?

Non-Returnable Items
  • Bodysuits, swimwear, lingerie, jewelry, beauty products, underwear, and event supplies once hygiene protection has been removed.
  • Cosmetics if the seal has been broken.
  • Items marked as non-returnable on the product page or checkout summary.
  • Free gifts when the qualifying item is kept and the return no longer meets the promotion rules.
  • Customized items and special made-to-order pieces.
Return requirements for returnable items:
Apparel, Dresses, Shoes and Accessories
  • Items must be unworn, undamaged, and free from makeup, perfume, stains, or pet hair.
  • Original tags, protective liners, boxes, and branded packaging should still be attached where relevant.
  • Any return that does not pass inspection may be sent back to the original shipping address.

  1. Sign into your Brand account.
  2. Go to My Orders and choose the order that contains the item(s) you want to return.
  3. Click Return, select the item(s), indicate the reason, and move to the next step.
  4. Select the return method. Availability may vary depending on your region and carrier restrictions.
  5. Choose your refund method and submit the request.
  6. In some cases, we may issue a refund without asking you to ship the item back.

Note:

  • If you want to return items from different orders, keep them separated unless your return portal specifically allows a combined return.
  • For oversized or overweight items, you may be asked to arrange return shipment yourself and keep your postage receipt.

5. How Do I Modify a Return Request?

Go to My Orders, open Returns, choose the relevant return record, then select Modify Return Items to add or remove products before the request is finalized.

6. How Do I Locate a Return Label/QR Code?

Go to My Orders, open the return request, and click Save Label or Show QR Code. The options available will depend on the return method selected in your region.

7. How Do I Cancel Return Request?

Open the same return request from your order history and select Cancel Return Request before the parcel has been scanned by the return carrier.

8. When will I get my refund and under what conditions?

Return Shipment Return Receipt Warehouse QC Refund Issued Financial Institution Process Time
5-7 days 1-3 days 3-5 days 1 day Usually 1-10 business days depending on your bank or payment provider.

When a returned item reaches our warehouse, it is inspected to confirm it still meets the return requirements. If the item is found to be worn, washed, damaged, missing tags, or otherwise unsuitable for resale, the refund may be declined.

  • The refund will be sent to your original payment method or store wallet, depending on the option you selected.
  • To avoid delays, make sure the items you return match the return record you created online.
  • If you receive a faulty or damaged item, contact Customer Service as soon as possible so we can review the case.
